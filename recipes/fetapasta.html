Feta Pasta

Back to recipes

A healthy hearty dish which is quick and easy to make.


You will need:

  • Pasta
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Feta Cheese
  • Olive oil

  1. Put on pasta to boil for 10 minutes.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, add all the other ingrediets to a large baking dish and cover with a little olive oil, bake at 180 degrees for 45 minutes.
  3. Drain the pasta and add it to the dish, mixing it up until an even coating of sauce.
