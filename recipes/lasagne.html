A delicious main, including beef, pasta, white sauce and tomato. Add cheese if you fancy.


You will need:

  • Pasta sheets
  • White Sauce
  • Beef Stock
  • Mushrooms
  • Passata
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Minced Beef
  • Onion

  1. Fry the beef, mushrooms, and onion in a skillet.
  2. When the onions and mushrooms have softened, add the beef stock, passata, and chopped tomatoes.
  3. Lay pasta sheets in a large dish and add alternate layers of white sauce, pasta sheets and the ingredients from the skillet as you fancy.
  4. Bake at 180 degrees for 45 minutes.
  5. (Optional) Once cooked, grate cheese on top and bake for an additional 5 minutes.
